Why Green Iron for Australia?

Australia is uniquely positioned to become a world leader in green iron production. Its natural endowments – abundant iron ore and a comparative advantage in low-cost renewable energy – make Australia the natural home for this emerging global industry. With soundly based policy settings and timely action, this opportunity can underpin prosperity for generations.

Economic Opportunity

Leveraging advantages in iron ore and renewables, Australia can move up the value chain. Potential to generate up to $386 billion annually by 2060.

Global Decarbonisation

An Australian green iron industry could abate emissions equal to roughly 4% of the global total – more than three times Australia's current domestic emissions.

Strategic Hedge

Investing in green iron safeguards national income and employment against the decline of fossil fuel exports in a decarbonising world.

  • Support common-user infrastructure (electricity transmission, hydrogen pipelines, ports).
  • Offer capital support for early commercial producers of green iron.

About The Superpower Institute

Founded in 2023 by economist Ross Garnaut and public policy expert Rod Sims, The Superpower Institute is a not-for-profit organisation dedicated to helping Australia seize the extraordinary economic opportunities of the post-carbon world.

The Institute's focus is on developing the policy settings, market incentives and practical knowledge necessary for Australia to become a major exporter of renewable energy and green industrial products.
